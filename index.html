Best ideas don't wait

- and neither does interesting information.

They appear during a walk, in class, or between meetings — and disappear just as fast.

We’re building a simple, distraction-free device to instantly capture thoughts and valuable information, so you can return to them later — organized and ready to use.
No noise. No competing apps. Just what matters, saved before it’s gone. It uses e-ink technology with stylus pen for a natural writing experience.

Pocketability

Thanks to its pocketable size, it's always within reach, ready for capturing the best thoughts.

Focus

It is a digital notebook designed just for managing your notes. Hereby, it offers a focused workspace with no distractions.

Unity

Unlike notes on scraps of paper or in several notebooks, this device offers a unified system allowing you to easily find and organize your notes in one dedicated place.
